Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use the translator?

1. Open a job card in ServiceM8

2. Click on the "Translate Message" action

3. Enter your message in the text area

4. Click "Translate Message"

5. Copy the translated text using the clipboard buttons

Which languages are supported?

We support over 100 languages including:

  • Spanish, French, German, Italian
  • Chinese (Simplified & Traditional), Japanese, Korean
  • Arabic, Hebrew, Russian, Portuguese
  • And many more!

The system automatically detects your input language.

Is my data secure?

Yes! We prioritize your privacy:

  • Messages are processed securely via encrypted connections
  • We don't store your translated content
  • All data is processed in real-time and discarded
  • GDPR compliant
What's the character limit?

Each message can be up to 1,000 characters long. This covers most business communications while ensuring fast, accurate translations.

How accurate are the translations?

We use OpenAI's GPT-4 for translations, providing:

  • Context-aware translations
  • Professional business tone
  • Industry-specific terminology recognition
  • High accuracy for common business scenarios
